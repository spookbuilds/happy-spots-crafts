Happy Spots Crafts

Colouring Book

Happy Spots is a wellbeing colouring book inspired by the wild beauty, rich history, and quiet magic of Northumberland and beyond. Within these pages you will find detailed illustrations filled with wildlife, foliage, and local history—each one designed to help you slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with a sense of calm.

Created by Northumberland-based artist and author Emma Arthur, Happy Spots invites you to lose yourself in places of peace and wonder. Whether you colour for mindfulness, creativity, or simple joy, this book offers a gentle escape into nature, heritage, and the restorative power of art.

Pick up your pencils, find your happy spot, and let the outside world fade away.
