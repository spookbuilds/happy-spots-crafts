About me

Hello, I'm Emma!

I was brought up mainly in Scotland but have lived in Northumberland for almost 20 years and take inspiration from the local coast, country and wildlife.

I have a grown up son and I live with my cat and dog who also provide a lot of inspiration for my art.

I create pet art, motor vehicles and more recently have created murals for local causes.

"Happy Spots" started in lockdown when a friend gifted me a sketchpad and I picked up my pencils to support my own mental health.
